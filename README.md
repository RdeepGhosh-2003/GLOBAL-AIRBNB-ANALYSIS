## 💡 Key Insights

📌 Paris leads in total listing volume across the analyzed cities.

📌 Cape Town records the highest average property prices.

📌 Superhosts consistently maintain stronger customer ratings than regular hosts.

📌 Host response rates positively correlate with guest satisfaction.

📌 Significant pricing variations exist across cities and neighborhoods.

📌 Geographic analysis reveals clear regional differences in listing density and pricing patterns.
